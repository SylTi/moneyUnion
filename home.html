What is MoneyUnion?

MoneyUnion is here to help you transmite any amount of money to friends, familly or merchants anywhere in 190 country (visa 50?) through text message, our smarphone web app or on a computer through this website. And our fee are very low : 0.5% on every transactions (+0.20$ when using our text-messaging service to cover for the extra cost

To give you a simple exemple : You live in New York and you want to send 1000$ back to your mother leaving in Kenya through usual mean like Western Union or MoneyGram it would take at least 5 days and 5% of the total amount.
By using our service she will receive 995$ a few seconds later on her text message capable phone. While you send it from the confort of your home with your computer, or from the subway with your smartphone.

Very low fee

Our leveraging of the Bitcoin network permit us to offer you very low fee : 0.5% for the sender, 0 % for the receiver.

Designed for users

We want to simplify your life as much as possible.

Locate Exchangers/Merchants

We help you find exchangers near you to exchange your Bitcoins for your local currency ; or directly help you find merchants accepting Bitcoin !

Text Message/Smartphone

Our platform is capable of supporting usage through Text Message, Smartphone App or our Website, each offering a simple and unique experience.

Safe

We safely store 100% of the Bitcoin of our user in hardware wallet as soon as we receive them. Most of them a safely stored inside the most secure builinds of all : banks.

Merchants solution

We offer multiple ways for merchants to receive Bitcoin payments. Both in store or online.
